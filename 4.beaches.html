UNAWATUNA

Unawatuna is a tropical beach located in south cost of the country. The beach has the best view of sunrise and that is the main tourist attraction in Unawatuna. The waves are so calm and relaxing and makes it easier to explore the underwater and the beautiful reefs.

ARU-GAM BAY

Aru-gam bay is located in the southern cost of Sri Lanka. At first the beach was a fishing village but now the beach is famous as one of the best surfing points across the world. The beach gives the opportunity to its visitors to admire the beauty of the sea through underwater photography.

KOGGALA

Koggala beach is one of the most beautiful beaches in Sri Lanka. There is a pleasant whether in Koggala during most time of the year. But the ideal time to Visit is between November to May when the sky is clear and have a really warm climate. The beach is only 20 minutes away from the Koggala town.
