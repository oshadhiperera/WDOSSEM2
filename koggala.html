KOGGALA

Koggala beach is one of the most beautiful beaches in Sri Lanka. There is a pleasant whether in Koggala during most time of the year. But the ideal time to Visit is between November to May when the sky is clear and have a really warm climate. The beach is only 20 minutes away from the Koggala town.
