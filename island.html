PIGEON ISLAND

Anyone visiting Trincomalee should pay a visit to the famous Pigeon Island. Some of Sri Lanka's best coral reefs and a profusion of fascinating marine life may be found in this stunning national park. Simply said, a trip here will make a lasting effect on you. Pigeon Island is about a kilometer off the shore of Nilaveli Beach, which is a beautiful location in its own right. The islands of Big Pigeon and Little Pigeon are really two separate islands. Both islands are named for the massive flocks of rock pigeons that nest among the islands' rough cliffs.
